SERVICES

VALUE ADDED SERVICES

Tailoring

We have custom orders for people who wish to alter their clothes to fit them better. Our delivery personnel have all the tools required to customize the apparel to fit your size. Please note that designs, textures and colours on the apparel can not be customized by our personanel. All customization is restricted to physical size and shape.


Exchanges and Returns

We offer a comprehensive design to accomodate all your tastes. In case you have a change of mind, do look at our exchange policy.The item must be in their original packaging and returned within 7 days,along with your receipt for either an exchange or a full refund. Refunds will be issued in the same method of payment as the original payment. Purchases made with cash: a cash refund will be issued. Purchases made with a credit card: credit will issued to the original card. Original card must be presented at time of return.

Free Delivery

For all the purchases you make in an excess of $100, we offer free delivery service. We will deliver at your door step free of charge(not applicable for internation orders). For all the other deliveries, a fixed delivery charge of $5 will be applicable.

Payment on delivery

Payment on Delivery option is available for a single item with a price limit of $50.For larger orders we would prefer you to first make payment thorugh any of the payment options available.The items will be delivered to you after the full payment is processed.
