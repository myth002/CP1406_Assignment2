Mission Statement:

The FRIAS website is intended to increase the revenue for FRIAS by promoting the services offered by the company, including the new CAFE. It intends to attract more number of people to their fashion shows, and also see an increase in the sale of their fashion products.

Success Evaluation

Other advertising will be consistent for a period of 3 months, so that any increase in sales of fashion wear should be attributable to the new website. At the end of this 3 month period, the number of people attending the fashion shows and the sales from the CAFE will be evaluated.

Target Audience:

Kids (of all ages) and their parents

  • Rich and Vibrant colors must be used to make the site visually appealing to kids

  • The design of the website must be descriptive and responsive to make purchasing easy and accessible to the parents.

Content:

  • Home (index) - Displays call-to-action statements and brief introduction of the company
  • About Us - Provides information about the company and it's mission
  • Products - Contains a catalogue of all our available fashion wear and their prices
  • Services - Provides information about Value Added Services and delivery details
  • Cafe - Provides information about the upcoming Cafe, such as the opening date and promotions
  • Events - Displays information about all upcoming events, such as fashion shows
  • F.A.Q - Contains answers to general queries and also information about company policies
  • Contact Us - Provides contact details of the company and also contains the feedback form
  • Order Now - Contains the order form that customers can use to place orders with
