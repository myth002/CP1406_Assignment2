FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

ORDERING

1. How do I make a purchase?

Once you have selected your desired product, click on the 'Order Now' button and follow the instructions to complete your purchase.

2. Can I place an order over the telephone?

Please call our Customer Care team or email for advice on how to place your order.

3. Do I need an account to place an order online?

No, you do not require an account on our website in order to place an order. You can fill up the Order Form to place your order instantly!

4. How will I know if you have received my order?

After you place your order, you will receive an email from us acknowledging that your order has been received.

5. What if an item is out of stock?

If items that you order are out of stock, we will try to contact you at the email address you provided when placing your order.

6. Do you offer same day delivery?

Yes, we do offer same day delivery. Find out more details about it on our Services page.

7. What happens if I notice that my personal details are incorrect after I have completed the ordering process?

If you realise your personal details are incorrect once you have completed the ordering process please email our customer service team at Frias@Gmail.com, with your order number.

8. Do Frias offer gift wrapping?

When placing your order, you can choose to have your items gift wrapped and to include an optional gift message.

RETURNS AND EXCHANGES

1. How do I receive a refund or exchange and how long does our Refund Policy last?

Unsuitable items may be refunded or exchanged using the same mode of payment and method of delivery as your purchase. You can request exchanges or refunds within 14 days of your purchase date.

2. When will I receive a refund for the returned items?

Refunds are processed on the day they are received and take 1-3 days to show in your account.

GENERAL QUESTIONS

1. How do I know if the item will fit me?

All our Fashion Wear products follow International Size Guidelines, have their respective sizes displayed on the catalogue.

2.Is my personal information kept private?

You can be assured that we consider all of the information you share with us are strictly private and confidential. At no point will FRIAS share your personal information without your consent.

3. Can you deliver to a different address than my billing address?

First time orders must be delivered to your billing address as stated on your Credit / Debit card for verification purposes. All subsequent orders can be placed to any location you desire.
